# InterflowAI Brand Layer

Use this when the user asks for "互通有无配色", "Interflow", "IP感", or brand-consistent article illustrations.

This is the default visual preset for this skill. The skill itself is general: users may replace this palette and byline with another brand system.

## Palette

Extracted from Interflow logo:

- Interflow Blue: `#0070DB` - primary brand color.
- Interflow Orange: `#FF5A00` - energetic signal / large abstract shape.
- Interflow Yellow: `#FFD200` - optimism / small circle / highlight.
- Interflow Mint: `#00E9A6` - connection / active dot / helper mark.
- Interflow Pink: `#FF978E` - warmth / human note / secondary blob.
- White: `#FFFFFF` - background and breathing room.
- Deep Navy/Black: near-black line art.

## Color Proportion

For white-background article illustrations:

- Keep the background white.
- Use deep navy/black for line art.
- Use Interflow Blue as the main fill color.
- Use Orange, Yellow, Mint, and Pink as recurring geometric accents.
- Let color blocks occupy roughly 20%-35% of the subject/object area when the user wants more IP feeling.
- Do not use the full logo as a background on every illustration.

## IP Feeling

Create brand recognition through a repeatable visual system, not through a pasted logo:

- recurring elongated human figures with expressive oversized hands
- floating Interflow-color geometric pieces: orange blob, yellow circle, mint dot, pink rounded block, blue card
- laptops/cards/folders as recurring work objects
- black/deep navy line art with white negative space
- playful off-balance poses that suggest "connecting", "matching", "translating", and "handing off"
- optional small byline/signature mark: `互通有无` or `Interflow`, placed in a quiet corner as a tiny editorial signature.

## Signature / Byline

Use a small signature when the user asks for 署名 or branded publishing assets:

- Preferred Chinese signature: `互通有无`
- Optional English signature: `Interflow`
- Placement: bottom-right or bottom-left corner, very small, like an editorial byline.
- Style: simple black/deep navy text, not the official logo, not a watermark, not a headline.
- Size: visually secondary; it should not compete with the illustration or labels.
- Use at most one signature per image.

## Interflow Character Logic

When drawing people for Interflow content:

- They are builders, connectors, translators, and organizers.
- They should look active and slightly overloaded, not polished corporate models.
- They carry questions, demos, cases, laptops, notes, and connection lines.
- They are human and warm, but not cute mascots.
- One or two people are allowed when the concept is about exchange, but avoid crowded group scenes unless requested.

## Hard Rules

- Do not recreate or distort the official Interflow logo unless the user explicitly asks for a cover using the logo.
- Do not put logo text in the image body. A tiny corner signature `互通有无` or `Interflow` is allowed when requested.
- Do not use the brand colors as random confetti only; each color should support hierarchy, rhythm, or recognition.
- Do not let colorful shapes overwhelm the article idea.
